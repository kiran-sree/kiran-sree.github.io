About us

Welcome to our page, where we tell the touching tale of our club and the difference we're doing in the lovely village of Karthikappally in the Indian state of Kerala. Our club has been committed to making the people we are connected with happy since its founding on February 5, 2012, while also making a significant contribution to the improvement of our neighbourhood. Join us as we examine the remarkable history of our club and the important job we accomplish.As we continue to create a better tomorrow one smile at a time, join us in our mission.
